What We Learned

  With the completion of this project, we've deepend our understanding of the Crisis magazine. It seems that one of the best ways to understand a time period is to see what people were talking about, which is exactly what our research on the Crisis expanded upon. It was used as a primary mode of message spreading during the Harlem Renaissance, but not just through text. Sure, we read articles, but there were stories, photographs, art, and even advertisements embedded in the Crisis. Therefore, as a team, by focusing on this one aspect of the Harlem Renaissance, we actually learned about many.
